*Il Nido* — the Nest. Tucked under original timber beams on the upper floor, this room has the quiet, enveloping quality of somewhere truly apart from the world.

A double bed in hand-carved walnut, stone walls, and a view over the courtyard fountain. Smaller than our other rooms, but not in character. Ideal for couples who prefer intimacy over scale.

Breakfast can be served in-room on request — a silver tray, a pot of coffee, and bread from the morning's delivery.
