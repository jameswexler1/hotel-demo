Step directly from your room onto a private stone-paved corner of the garden. *Camera del Giardino* is our ground-floor room — simpler, closer to the earth, and with a particular charm at dawn.

A double bed with organic linen, whitewashed walls, and French doors opening to your own private patch of the rose garden. Breakfast is served outside at your table in season.

Our most affordable room, and for some guests — the most beloved.
