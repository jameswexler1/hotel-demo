*Suite Corte* occupies the best corner of the house — 48 square metres arranged as a king bedroom, a separate sitting room furnished with antiques, and a sweeping private terrace overlooking both garden and countryside.

The bathroom centres on an original cast-iron clawfoot tub. Morning papers, evening turn-down, a Nespresso machine, and minibar stocked with Franciacorta are all included.

For anniversaries, honeymoons, and occasions that deserve more than the ordinary. Accommodates up to three guests.
