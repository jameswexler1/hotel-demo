Named for the climbing roses that frame its window, *La Stanza delle Rose* is the room that gave our hotel its name. Soft morning light filters through antique linen curtains, carrying the scent of the garden.

The king-size bed is dressed in 100% Egyptian cotton. The stone-floored bathroom features a walk-in shower with rainfall head and handmade Lombardy soap. A curated selection of local wines waits in the minibar.

Wake to birdsong and the sight of rose-covered walls. This room books out first — we recommend reserving early.
