*La Loggia* takes its name from the covered terrace that extends the room into the open Lombard sky. Mornings here begin with espresso and the sight of vineyard hills rolling to the horizon.

Inside, a freestanding bathtub in the bathroom is positioned to face the terrace doors — soak while the sun sets behind the hills. The king bed, reclaimed oak floors, and the scent of wisteria complete the picture.

Our most-requested room. Sunsets from the terrace are, guests tell us, genuinely unforgettable.
