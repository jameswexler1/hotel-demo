Every morning at 9:00 AM, a guide leads a gentle 22km cycle from the hotel through vines, past the village of Erbusco, and along the southern shore of Lake Iseo before looping back.

The route is flat and unhurried. Classic Italian bicycles are provided — no lycra required. The ride ends with a cold Franciacorta Rosé waiting in the courtyard.

Suitable for all fitness levels. Helmets provided. Minimum 2 guests.
