Franciacorta is Italy's answer to Champagne — and we sit at its heart. Our partner estates are a 15-minute drive from the hotel, and our sommelier has hand-selected the best for an unhurried morning of tasting.

Six wines. A tour of the historic cellars. Paired antipasti. And conversation about why Lombard bubbles have, for those who know, surpassed the French originals.

Transport from and to the hotel is included. The experience begins at 10:00 AM and returns for a late lunch. Private group bookings available.
